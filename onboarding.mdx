## Complete onboarding in 2 steps

The Tenantum onboarding form is simple: just enter your **basic info** and **regional preferences** to personalize your account.

---

### Step 1: Basic Info

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user" title="Basic Information">
    Fill out your profile details:
    - **Full Name**  
    - **Email Address** (pre-filled from sign-up)  
    - **Phone Number** (optional)  

    <Tip>This info appears in your account header and tenant-facing communications.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

### Step 2: Regional & Timezone

## Get started in three steps

Set up your Tenantum account, add your first property, and start tracking rent.

---

### Step 1: Create Your Account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Sign up for Tenantum">
    1. Go to [app.tenantum.com](https://app.tenantum.com).  
    2. Enter your name, email, and secure password.  
    3. Verify your email to activate your account.  

    <Tip>Your dashboard will open automatically after email verification.</Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="user" title="Complete your profile">
    - Add your phone number and landlord/company details.
    - Upload your company logo (optional) to personalize your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

### Step 2: Add Your First Property

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="house" title="Create a property">
    - Go to **Properties → Add Property**  
    - Enter property name, address, and type (e.g., Apartment, House).  
    - Add property photos for easy identification.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="building" title="Set up units (if applicable)">
    - For multi-unit properties, add each unit:  
      - Unit number/name  
      - Size or type (e.g., 2-bedroom)  
      - Rent price  
    - Mark units as vacant or occupied.  
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

### Step 3: Add Tenants & Record Rent

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="id-card" title="Add a tenant">
    - Go to **Tenants → Add Tenant**  
    - Enter tenant name, email, and phone number.  
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="file-contract" title="Create a lease">
    - Assign tenant to a unit.  
    - Set lease start and end dates.  
    - Enter rent amount and payment frequency (e.g., monthly).  
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="money-bill" title="Record your first payment">
    - Navigate to **Payments → Record Payment**.  
    - Select tenant, input payment details, and save.  
    - Tenant’s balance updates automatically.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
